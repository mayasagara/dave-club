Book spotlight

Promise at Dawn by Romain Gary (published in 1960)

Promise at Dawn by Romain Gary is an autobiographical novel that recounts the author’s childhood and youth under the powerful influence of his mother, a fiercely ambitious woman who is determined that her son will achieve greatness. Following their life from Eastern Europe to France, the narrative traces his development into a writer and war hero, particularly during his service as a pilot in World War II. Both humorous and deeply moving, the novel is a tribute to maternal love and its lasting impact on a man’s identity and destiny.
